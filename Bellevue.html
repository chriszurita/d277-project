BELLEVUE

Bellevue, Washington, balances modernity with tranquility, with skyscrapers against serene parks. Bellevue Square offers upscale boutiques and gourmet dining, while Bellevue Arts Museum celebrates creativity. Tech companies flourish, and green spaces abound, from Cougar Mountain Regional Wildland Park to Lake Washington waterfront strolls. Bellevue blends urban amenities with natural beauty, offering a refined lifestyle in the Pacific Northwest.

  • Incorporated: 1953
  • Region: Estside Puget Sound
  • Classification: Urban
  • Median Income: 73,892 USD (2022)
