SEATTLE

Seattle, Washington, is a vibrant city renowned for its captivating blend of natural beauty, innovative culture, and progressive spirit. Home to the iconic Space Needle piercing the skyline, it boasts a captivating waterfront and breathtaking mountain vistas. Seattle's cultural tapestry is rich, with world-class museums like the Museum of Pop Culture and the Seattle Art Museum. Coffee culture thrives in this city, birthplace of Starbucks, while Pike Place Market buzzes with activity, offering fresh produce, local crafts, and lively street performances.

  • Incorporated: 1869
  • Region: Southeastern Puget Sound
  • Classification: Urban
  • Median income: 63,167 USD (2022)
  • WA State average income: 90,325 USD (2022)
